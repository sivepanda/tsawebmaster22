Lendric Kalemar

Inspiring his Hometown

Kalemar is a 36 year-old rapper from Chicago. After growing up in complete poverty, Kalemar grew around a multitude of negative influences, and while he was known for the occasional fight, Kalemar was also known as a child genius, maintaining a perfect GPA, 15 different AP, SAT and ACT scores. Majoring in theoretical physics and chemistry, Kalemar spent his early adult years in the military, where he became the leading nuclear and chemical scientist in the defense laboratories, working in secret in Area 51. Soon, though, he returned home to Chicago to become a professor at the University of Chicago, while also taking up rapping about his struggles as a teenager and his ability to retain his morals through his parents and family, and spreading his come-up story to inspire other teens his age.
