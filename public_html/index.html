THE EVENTS

NU_WRLD has a wide selection of ways you can attend events. You can opt for a more traditional 2D streaming affair while still being able to take advantage of our room-filling and revolutionary Intillis Novis video and audio codecs, or you can opt for a more immersive 3D experience, allowing you to fully indulge yourself in the creative mindsets of your favorite artists. We do this at no overly inflated cost. We make sure our event prices are always accessible for every price point, and that every event tier is fairly and competitively priced. We enable you to attend events for a price far more affordable than a physical festival, while still delivering a similar experience.

THE ARTISTS

With many artists bringing talent from around the globe, NU_WRLD offers a truly connecting experience, allowing artists from all over the world to connect with fans located on all corners of the globe. This allows for meaningful and valuable experiences to be equally accessible to anyone with an internet connection, regardless of location. Not only that, our dedicated team of software engineers and graphics designers ensure that the full scope of an artist’s vision is realized. We bring together critically acclaimed international artists who otherwise may have a difficult time expanding their reach, give them a platform and tools to expand their reach and creative vision, all to bring you an event of a lifetime.
