The Roster

S’more

A fan of Marshmallows (and a certain artist with that very name), S’more focuses on features from other artists and lyrics in her music along with the beats she produces. Unlike her idol, however, she shows her identity and shares personal experiences quite frequently such as those that made her pursue this genre. She has charted #1 on charts several times as well as earning a Grammy, the very first Vietnamese artist to do so for the Electronic genre.

Paft Drunk

Big supporters of the former duo Daft Punk, Paft Drunk burst into the scene in 2015, 5 years before Daft Punk had mysteriously left the scene without revealing anything. Paft Drunk is actually a band consisting of 5 members from France, Indonesia, and Germany that met over a virtual group therapy session while suffering from a drinking problem. Since then, they have charted numerous times and, like DROOLE, will be performing their 3rd album, Dwanye.

Bassthoven

A 54-year-old Korean artist who goes by the name of Bassthoven. His music is quite distinct from other electronica artists as it connects the old classical theme with the more recent ambient electronic music of today. Although an avid supporter of the revival of classical music, he was introduced to electronica through his son the summer he lost his woodworking job due to the pandemic. His first album was released 2 weeks ago and his first major concert will be with us!

SAZ

A 25 year old Pakistani American who didn’t succumb to the pressures of his culture’s tolerance towards music. He made a name for himself by becoming the first Pakistani to hit top 10 on International charts by making a track that is both hype and relatable - not only to his home Pakistani culture, but also his secondary American culture.

Dfrog

A 17-year-old electric bass and double bass player, Dfrog creates electronic based tracks. His music is heavily influenced by progressive rock and metal - especially from bands like Haken, Creo, and Dream Theater, with some classical influence as well. He aims to infuse progressive ideas into his electronic music.

Soundcloud

Galaxies

Galaxies is a 16 year-old producer who works heavily with the more technological side of electronic music. A friend of Dfrog, he also works on doing graphic design for both himself and Dfrog. As such, he makes the imagery and storytelling apparent in his music, despite the instrumental nature of his tracks.

Soundcloud

Parum Cattus

A lover of languages and cultures (earning degrees in Ethnicity, Race & Migration, History, Humanities, and Global Affairs from Yale), Cattus incorporates several artists from all over the world in his music regularly. He has ranked in 3 in 5 countries globally (including a worldwide superhit produced alongside SAZ). Cactus's music consists of everything from trance, to rap, to classical music, and his professional music training in various countries ensures that, no matter what music he makes or where he makes it, it is true to the original culture.

Sleep Stage

A progressive metal band based in Lincolnshire, UK, Sleep Stage is not afraid to experiment with new and interesting beats, many of which are widely considered unorthodox, and is globally prized for their uniqueness and creativity. Sleep Stage is composed of 8 individuals who met during their children’s elementary school play. They began developing a completely unique brand of music, marked by their wacky time signatures and their unique and diverse instrument array.

Lendric Kalemar

A 36 year-old rapper from Chicago. While up in complete poverty, Kalemar was known as child genius, maintaining a perfect GPA, 15 different AP, SAT and ACT scores despite his circumstances. Kalemar spent his early adult years in the military, where he became the leading nuclear and chemical scientist in the defense laboratories, working in secret in Area 51. Soon, though, he returned home to Chicago to become a professor at the University of Chicago, taking up rapping about his struggles as a teenager and his ability to retain his morals through his parents and family, and spreading his come-up story to inspire teens going through similar struggles.

DaSenior

69 year old rapper from Los Angeles. He was everything during his life, everything unsuccessful. A waiter, clerk, and even a swim coach. He tried to make ends meet despite not being interested in his jobs. However, that all changed when he discovered the art of rapping. He fell in love with it and got himself a few gigs. At this time he was nearly 60 years old. Many may think he doesn’t have anything special to rap about, but he used his age to answer. Nonetheless, he became even more popular across the country in various age groups.

Ivory Vibe

Oregon’s leading grunge band, following in the footsteps of other popular Pacific Northwest grunge groups, Igneous Vibe (later renamed Ivory Vibe) emerged in 1993 out of Portland, Oregon and remained relatively unknown until 2021, when their single “You Ate My Burgers” became an Internet meme on the short-form video platform TikTok. From there, their fanbase has exploded, and continues to grow to this day. Ivory Vibe is set to release their fifth studio album, “IVory Dreams” with the help of NU_WRLD.

Droole

A very private individual, DROOLE’s name, age and other info has been kept secret to the public. It is, however, speculated that they hail from the Netherlands. Their style focuses more on a strict 2013 dubstep (with elements of the more modern wave genre) sound with little to no lyrics as he deems them “unnecessary” to making a good song. Debuting in 2014, he has featured in several video games and sci-fi films. Rarely releasing music, this will be a performance of their third, and most recent, album, Only Through My Eyes - released just last year.
