Our Clubs

TSA

Advisor: Susan James, Location: room 110

TSA is a leadership-based technology, art, and STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, Math, and Science) club with a vast array of events and interests that it caters to. Competitions begin at a regional level, then build to state and national-level conferences, each packed with many opportunities to learn, build, and lead.

DECA

Advisor: Christy Flanigan, Location: contact sponsor

DECA is a leadership-based buisiness-focused club. It is designed to further students' knowledge and experience in the field of business, marketing, finance, and leadership and prepare participants for leadership positions.

Robotics

Advisor: Cheri & Randy Blackwood, Location: contact sponsors

Robotics is a club based on STEM concepts, and furthers students' knowlege in coding, engineering, aand design. They compete in various competitions, such as the FIRST Robotics Competition. Students will be able to build and design robots to certain specifications, and then program them to complete certain tasks.

Academic Team

Advisor: Sony Lovell, Location: contact sponsors

Academic Team is a club based on knowledge, team dynamic, and competition. Students are able to compete in quiz bowl-type competitions across the state. They compete in competitions such as NAQT, Froshmore (9th and 10th grades ONLY), and more.
