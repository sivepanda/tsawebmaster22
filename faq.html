Have Questions? We've got you.

As far as the display goes, it depends on the package you purchased. Generally, you will be using your own personal display. If you buy our VR package, you will be sent a VR headset of your choice. The creative process of the concert will depend entirely on the artist, however.

The timing of when an event goes live depends on the artist and when they decide to make the information public, however, through our catalog of artists you can select the ones you would like to be alerted about and will receive a notification whenever they decide to have an event.

Through us you will be able to experience any digitized music event commissioned to us by an artist. We are truly global meaning you can watch an event from anywhere with high quality and amazing sound, using our state-of-the-art lossless streaming codecs.
