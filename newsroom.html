We are excited to announce that we will be hosting events every Friday, Saturday and Sunday. Alongside our usual acts, we will begin allowing DJs to submit sets that, upon acceptance, they will play at one, or many of our events!

In partnership with our sister company, ADO Technologies, we are happy to announce our new VR streaming capabilities, available with the Pro tier of events. We will be streaming both the event and audio in a 3D space, making you feel more connected and immersed in the event than ever before. Along with this, we also announce our new custom VR headset, Canary, with significantly higher performing audio and visual systems to take full advantage of Intilis Novis.

Three new acclaimed artists have now joined our roster. SAZ, Galaxies, and Dfrog have joined us and will be part of future events.

With the newfound seriousness of the pandemic, it is refreshing to see our event being one that provides a glimpse into how we can continue into the coming months of uncertainty while still keeping ourselves entertained. The success of this event seems to bode well for our future, and we are excited to see what future events bring.

We have decided not to cancel our March 13th event, however, we will work to make sure our artists will be safe. Unfortunately our crews will not be dispatched to help the artists set up. We are still working virtually with artists to make sure that everything is up to our standards, and that we will still be able to provide an amazing show.

COVID-19 has recently emerged, and we are taking precautions to make sure our employees are as safe as possible. Over the past two months, many of our global offices, including our East Asian, North American, South American, and European locations. We continue to operate strictly necessary server maintenance in-person in our African offices, and we are preparing to transition to a virtually operated business over the next week. As we continue to monitor the COVID-19 situation, we will make a decision regarding the March 13th event over the coming weeks.
